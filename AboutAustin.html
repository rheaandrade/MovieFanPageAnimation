Austin Abrams aka Ben



Austin:

Austin Abrams (born September 2, 1996) is an American actor, known for playing Pete in Gangster Squad (2013), Aaron in The Kings of Summer (2013), Ben Starling in Paper Towns (2015), and Ron Anderson in The Walking Dead.

Ben:

One of Quentin and Radar's best friends. Travels with them and his girlfriend, Lacey. Had a story spread about him called "Bloody Ben" when Becca said that he has blood in his urine due to chronic masturbation, when in reality it was just a kidney infection. Holds keg-stand record, peed in many bottles on the journey to Agloe, New York, and saved the lives of himself, Radar, Lacey, and Quentin when the car was about to crash into an enormous white cow.
