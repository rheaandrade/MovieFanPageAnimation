Caitlin Carver aka Baca



Caitlin:

Caitlin Carver (born 1992) is an American actress and dancer.Carver was born in Monrovia, Alabama. She attended Sparkman High School in Harvest, Alabama where she graduated in 2010. At the age of two, she started to dance until eventually she began theater in high school. She has worked as a background dancer with many notable recording artists in the music industry including Beyoncé, Ne-Yo, and Pitbull.
